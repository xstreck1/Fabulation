The game is for 3-9 players (strictly multiplayer).

This is a storytelling game.

Each round you are given a sentence with empty parts, symbolized by "...".

Your task is to make up a part of a story by filling the "..." in a time limit.

After that you pass on the device.

You are also allowed to change the gender of what you are given and change between singular and plural.

After each round each player picks theirs favourites from the last round.

After the last round, the player who got the most points becomes the author of the story.
